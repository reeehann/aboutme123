My Story

Who I Am

Growing up in the vibrant capital city of Malé, Maldives, I've always been surrounded by beauty — from the turquoise ocean to the energy of island life. That environment shaped my eye for aesthetics and my love for capturing moments through photography. Today, I channel that creative instinct into marketing, studying at Maldives National University (MNU) where I'm learning how brands tell stories, connect with audiences, and drive real impact in the digital age. I believe great marketing, like a great photograph, is all about perspective — and I'm here to bring a fresh one.
